Claims with dates 
#################

Dates, specifically time-bound claims, are the central focus of the Security Force Monitor's data and methodology. Sometimes the dates for claims are clearly stated in citations and the methodology for structuring these claims into time-bound data is simple. Often, however, citations contain ambigious claims about dates which need careful consideration to properly capture as data.
