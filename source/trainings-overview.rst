Overview: Trainings (Draft)
###########################

.. warning::

   Training is a claim format currently in development. The data structure described in this documentation is a proposed format which may change with further research and development.


Trainings are events where the security forces of one country are trained by another country (domestic training activities are not a focus of research currently). The focus of trainings may be on law and policy, weapons usage, technical skills, or other sorts of training. The number of trainees may range from a single person to hundreds of personnel.

Data about trainings are captured in single claim type (this may increase with additional development):

 - :ref:`training`: this claim type records basic information about a training event including its location, the trainer and trainee units, the cost of the training and the dates it occurred.
